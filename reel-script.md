# Instagram Reel Script — "I Made an AI That Judges UFC Fights"

**Length:** ~30 sec, talking to camera + screen recording
**Vibe:** casual, hype, like you're telling a friend

---

## HOOK (0–3s)
> "I made an AI that judges UFC fights better than the actual judges."

## THE RELATABLE BIT (3–10s)
> "You know how everyone's always mad the judges got a fight wrong? Yeah. So I basically built a robot referee that never gets it wrong."

## HOW IT WORKS — keep it simple (10–20s)
> "It's like ChatGPT, but instead of reading your messages, it watches the fight. It sees every punch, every takedown, every second one guy is in control — and it scores the round for you, live."

## THE DEMO (20–28s) — show the scorecard on screen
> "I just point it at the fight, and boom — it fills out the scorecard in real time. This round? 10–9. And it doesn't have a favorite fighter, so it's actually fair."

## CTA (28–32s)
> "Follow for part 2 — I'll show you it calling a whole fight."

---

## On-screen captions
- 0s: "AI that judges UFC fights 🥊"
- 5s: "Because the real judges keep messing up 😤"
- 12s: "It literally WATCHES the fight 👀"
- 22s: "Live scorecard, no bias"
- 28s: "Follow for part 2 →"

## Shots to film
1. You talking to camera (hook)
2. Fight playing on your screen
3. The scorecard updating live
4. Close-up on a "10–9" score

## Tips
- Trending hype audio, beat drop around the demo (~20s)
- Fast cuts, keep energy high
- No tech jargon — if grandma can't follow it, cut it
